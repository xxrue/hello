üìù Complete Example

Topic: Should schools have longer recess times?

Opinion Statement:

"I strongly believe that schools should extend recess time from 20 minutes to at least 40 minutes because it benefits students' physical health, mental wellbeing, and social development."

Main Point 1: Physical Health ‚ñº

Firstly, longer recess times promote physical health among students. Students spend 6-7 hours sitting in classrooms daily. A longer recess allows them to engage in physical activities like playing futsal or badminton at the school field, which helps combat childhood obesity‚Äîa growing concern where studies show 30% of children are overweight.

Main Point 2: Mental Wellbeing ‚ñº

Secondly, extended recess improves students' mental wellbeing and focus. After intensive morning classes in subjects like Mathematics and Science, students need adequate time to relax their minds. When I have a longer break, I return to class feeling refreshed and ready to concentrate. Research shows that breaks improve cognitive performance and reduce stress levels among students.

Main Point 3: Social Development ‚ñº

Thirdly, longer recess periods enhance social development. Schools are multicultural environments where students from different backgrounds learn together. Longer recess periods provide valuable opportunities for students to build friendships across cultural boundaries, practice communication skills, and develop important social values that reflect our spirit of unity.

Conclusion:

"In conclusion, extending recess time in schools is not merely about giving students more playtime‚Äîit is an investment in their holistic development. By providing adequate time for physical activity, mental rest, and social interaction, we prepare our students to become healthier, happier, and more well-rounded individuals."
